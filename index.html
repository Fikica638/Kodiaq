1.5 TSI Mild Hybrid Petrol: This 150 hp (110 kW) engine features mild-hybrid technology, enhancing fuel efficiency and reducing emissions. Paired with a 7-speed DSG transmission, it delivers a balanced performance ideal for urban and highway driving.


2.0 TSI Petrol: For those seeking more power, the 2.0-liter TSI engine produces 204 hp (150 kW) and comes with all-wheel drive and a DSG transmission, offering a dynamic and responsive driving experience.


2.0 TDI Diesel: Available in two variants, this engine provides either 150 hp (110 kW) with front-wheel drive or 193 hp (142 kW) with all-wheel drive. Both versions are equipped with a 7-speed DSG, ensuring efficient and smooth performance, particularly suited for long-distance travel.


1.5 TSI Plug-in Hybrid (PHEV): Combining a 1.5-liter TSI petrol engine with an electric motor, this setup delivers a total system output of 204 hp (150 kW). It features a 25.7 kWh battery, enabling an electric-only range of over 62 miles (100 kilometers), making it an excellent choice for eco-conscious drivers.

The 2024 Škoda Kodiaq offers a range of engines, each delivering distinct performance characteristics:

1.5 TSI Mild Hybrid Petrol: This engine produces 150 hp (110 kW), achieving 0-62 mph (0-100 km/h) in approximately 9.7 seconds, with a top speed of 129 mph (208 km/h).

2.0 TSI Petrol: Delivering 204 hp (150 kW), it accelerates from 0-62 mph in around 7.7 seconds, reaching a top speed of approximately 130 mph (210 km/h).

2.0 TDI Diesel: The 193 hp (142 kW) variant achieves 0-60 mph in 7.4 seconds, with a top speed of about 130 mph (210 km/h).

1.5 TSI Plug-in Hybrid (PHEV): Combining a petrol engine with an electric motor for a total of 204 hp (150 kW), it accelerates from 0-62 mph in 8.4 seconds, with a top speed of 130 mph (209 km/h).
